INSECT HOUSE

Enter the fascinating world of creepy crawlies! The Insect House is home to a wide variety of insects, from colorful butterflies to hard-shelled beetles.

What you can explore:

  • Butterfly Dome
  • Ant Colony Maze
  • Beetle Showcase
  • Interactive Spider Exhibit

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Insect House is located just north of the Coffee Shop, next to the Lost Forest trail.
