LOST FOREST

Step into the mysterious Lost Forest and explore nature in its most untouched form. This winding forest trail is full of surprises and perfect for adventurous souls.

Things to discover:

  • Hidden treehouse viewpoints
  • Exotic plants and towering trees
  • Birdwatching platforms
  • Quiet nature paths and photo spots

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest is located to the east of the Insect House, just beyond the Garden Amphitheatre.
